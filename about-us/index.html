    About Us

    Scorpion Chemical Factory LLC is one of the leading distributors of Automobile Batteries in Oman established in 1975. The company has an efficient sales team that ventures through the interior markets all over the country. The company offers Indian made batteries with the brand name of ADDO PLATINUM and Oman Origin Batteries with the brand name of 'Antara'. Scorpion Chemical factory is the supplier of a whole range of batteries, starting from small-sized Car Batteries to big Trucks Batteries (both Dry Charged and Maintenance free). Recently we started to distribute AGM Batteries & Grand star EFB Batteries of Antara.
