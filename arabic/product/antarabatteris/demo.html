Grandstar AGM

Product Overview

Grandstar AGM batteries are high end automotive batteries which are rapidly rising to the top of the battery market. Noble metal electrode technology is used. AGM batteries are valve-regulated, lead-acid (VRLA) battery. AGM batteries last long, It can provide a significant power spike even when they are exhausted.

Key Features

  • Sealed and maintenance free
  • Absorbent Glass Mat(AGM) Technology
  • Superior Vibration resistance
  • High Power Performance
  • Spill-proof design
  • Reduced gassing(VRLA)
  • Suitable for advanced automotive features
  • Quick recharge capability
